Estimate long-distance travel time and charging behavior for an electric vehicle with configurable speed, battery, consumption, and charging curve parameters.

The consumption curve is approximated with a quadratic function based on the consumption values at 100 km/h and 120 km/h.
The charging curve is approximated with a cubic function based on the charging times from 20% to 80% and from 80% to 100% state of charge.
